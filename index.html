Chocklet Lava cake 

   
    
A rich, gooey chocolate cake with
 a molten center —
restaurant-quality, made at home.

        Ingredients

        


            

                1/2 cup unsalted butter
            


            

                4 oz dark chocolate, chopped
            


            

                1 cup powdered sugar
            


            

                2 large eggs
            


            

                2 large egg yolks
            


            

                1/2 tsp vanilla extract
            


            

                1/4 cup all-purpose flour
            


            

                1 pinch salt
            


            

                1 tbsp cocoa powder, for dusting ramekins
            


        


    






        



    

        step-by-step Instruction

        


            

                1
Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Butter and dust four ramekins with cocoa powder 
            


            

                2 Melt butter and dark chocolate together in a heatproof bowl until smooth and glossy.
            


            

                3 Whisk in powdered sugar until fully combined. 
            


            

                 4 Add eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract, whisking until the batter is smooth 
            


            

                5 Gently fold in flour and a pinch of salt until just combined — do not overmix.
            


            

                6 Divide batter evenly among the ramekins and place them on a baking sheet.
            


            

                7 Bake for 12 minutes, until the edges are set but the center still jiggles slightly. 
            


            

                8 Let cool for 1 minute, then invert onto plates. Dust with powdered sugar and serve immediately.
